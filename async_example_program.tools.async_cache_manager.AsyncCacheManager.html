Constructor: AsyncCacheManager(offline_path)

View In Hierarchy

Class that handles archiving and restoring of supplied active caches.

After you have called the restore_cache() method, you have to call the clear_restored_cache() method, so that the cache files are deleted.

The reason for splitting it into two methods is that something might go wrong in your application, at least in the early stages and then the data would be gone forever (sometimes safety is better in foresight than hindsight).
